A Secure and Scalable
Blockchain Development Platform

Adamnite is a layer-1 blockchain development platform that enables
developers easily to build efficient and secure decentralized applications. Developers can leverage Adamnite's in-built knowledge verification software to dynamically check their smart contracts for logical invalidites, and have faith that Adamnite's DPOS consensus mechanism will support their dApp long-term once it has been deployed to mainnet.

Decentralized Autonomous
Organizations (DAOs)

Crypto Game
Infrastructure

Use Adamnite's Knowledge Verification Software to power a gaming application that runs fully on-chain, with player behavior, incentives, and attributes all determined/stored by smart contracts that are computationally tested.

Social Networks

Video Encoding and Streaming

Encode video bytes directly in transactions on the Adamnite blockchain, allowing you to stream to anyone with an internet connection.

until certain requirements have been met.
